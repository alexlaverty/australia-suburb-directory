Facts about COOKTOWN

  1. Cooktown is located at the mouth of the Endeavour River.
  2. It is the site where Captain James Cook beached the Endeavour for repairs in 1770.
  3. Cooktown is the northernmost town on the east coast of Australia accessible by sealed road.
  4. The town's name comes from Captain James Cook.
  5. Cooktown boasts the first permanent European settlement in Queensland.
  6. The area is known for its rich Indigenous Australian history.
  7. Cooktown is a popular destination for fishing enthusiasts.
  8. The Cooktown Orchid is Queensland's floral emblem.
  9. The town has a diverse range of flora and fauna.
  10. Cooktown holds an annual Discovery Festival re-enacting Cook's landing.
