Facts about WOOLMAR

  1. Woolmar is a rural locality in the Scenic Rim Region, Queensland, Australia.
  2. It is located approximately 100km southwest of Brisbane.
  3. The area is known for its agricultural land use.
  4. The local economy is primarily based on farming and grazing.
  5. Woolmar is within the Fassifern Valley.
  6. The region experiences a humid subtropical climate.
  7. The name "Woolmar" is believed to be derived from a local property name.
  8. The area is part of the traditional lands of the Yugambeh people.
  9. The town has a small permanent population.
  10. Woolmar was once serviced by a railway line, which has since been closed.
