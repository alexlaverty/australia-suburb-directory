Facts about CLIFTON

  1. Clifton is located in the Darling Downs region of Queensland, Australia.
  2. The town is situated on the banks of the Condamine River.
  3. Clifton is known for its rich agricultural land, particularly for wheat and other grains.
  4. The town was established in the 1860s.
  5. Clifton is home to a number of heritage-listed buildings.
  6. The Clifton Co-operative Society, established in 1893, is one of the oldest and largest agricultural cooperatives in Australia.
  7. Clifton hosts an annual show, showcasing local agricultural produce and livestock.
  8. The town has a number of parks and gardens, including the Clifton Recreation Reserve.
  9. Clifton is a popular destination for fishing and other water-based activities.
  10. The town is located approximately 180km south-west of Brisbane.
