Facts about BOULIA

  1. Boulia is located in the Channel Country of Central West Queensland.
  2. The town is situated on the Burke River.
  3. Boulia is the administrative centre of the Shire of Boulia.
  4. The area is known for its Min Min lights, a mysterious unexplained nocturnal light phenomenon.
  5. Boulia holds an annual camel race, the Boulia Desert Sands.
  6. The town experiences a hot desert climate.
  7. Dinosaur fossils have been discovered in the region around Boulia.
  8. The town has a small airport, Boulia Airport.
  9. Boulia is known for its cattle grazing.
  10. The traditional owners of the land are the Pitta Pitta Aboriginal people.
