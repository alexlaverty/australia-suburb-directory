Facts about TAMARIND GARDENS

  1. Tamarind Gardens is a residential suburb located in Logan City, Queensland, Australia.
  2. It is situated approximately 27 kilometers south of the Brisbane CBD.
  3. The suburb is known for its quiet, family-friendly atmosphere.
  4. Tamarind Gardens is primarily made up of detached houses, with some townhouses and units also present.
  5. The area features several parks and green spaces, providing recreational opportunities for residents.
  6. There are a number of schools and childcare centers located in or near Tamarind Gardens.
  7. The suburb is well-connected to other parts of Logan City and Brisbane via public transport.
  8. Shopping centers and other amenities are readily accessible from Tamarind Gardens.
  9. The median house price in Tamarind Gardens has generally shown steady growth over the past years.
  10. The suburb falls under the local government area of Logan City Council.
