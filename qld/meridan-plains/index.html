Facts about MERIDAN PLAINS

  1. Meridan Plains is located in the Sunshine Coast Region of Queensland, Australia.
  2. It is situated within the broader urban area of Caloundra.
  3. The suburb experienced significant growth in the 2000s and 2010s.
  4. Meridan Plains is primarily a residential area.
  5. The name 'Meridan' is derived from an Aboriginal word meaning 'place of sand'.
  6. The plains were traditionally used by the local Aboriginal people for hunting and gathering.
  7. Meridan Plains is home to a number of parks and reserves.
  8. The suburb is well-connected to surrounding areas by road.
  9. Several schools are located in or near Meridan Plains.
  10. Shopping facilities are available within the suburb and in nearby areas.
