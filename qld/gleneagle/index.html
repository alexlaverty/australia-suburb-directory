Facts about GLENEAGLE

  1. Gleneagle is located in the Scenic Rim Region of Queensland, Australia.
  2. It is situated approximately 68 kilometers south of Brisbane.
  3. The suburb is known for its semi-rural atmosphere and scenic views.
  4. Gleneagle borders the Mount Barney National Park, a popular destination for bushwalking and hiking.
  5. The area experiences a humid subtropical climate.
  6. Gleneagle is part of the Beaudesert Shire Council.
  7. Residential development in Gleneagle began in the late 20th century.
  8. The suburb is primarily residential with a mix of acreage properties and smaller housing blocks.
  9. Gleneagle State School is the primary school serving the suburb.
  10. Local businesses in Gleneagle primarily cater to the local community.
