Facts about CARPENDALE

  1. Carpendale is located approximately 12km west of Brisbane CBD.
  2. The suburb is primarily residential with a mix of housing styles.
  3. Carpendale is known for its leafy streets and established gardens.
  4. The suburb is bordered by the Brisbane River.
  5. Carpendale is part of the Centenary Ward.
  6. The median house price in Carpendale is generally higher than the Brisbane average.
  7. Carpendale is within close proximity to Indooroopilly Shopping Centre.
  8. The suburb falls within the catchment area for several reputable schools.
  9. Carpendale has good access to public transport, including bus and train services.
  10. The suburb is relatively quiet and family-friendly.
