Facts about WERANGA

  1. Weranga is located in the Goondiwindi Region of Queensland, Australia.
  2. The town is situated on the western Darling Downs.
  3. The area is primarily agricultural, with farming being a significant industry.
  4. Weranga is located near the Moonie River.
  5. The town has a history associated with the development of the Surat Basin's resources.
  6. Weranga is located approximately 440 kilometers southwest of Brisbane.
  7. The town is served by the Weranga State School.
  8. Weranga experiences a hot and dry climate.
  9. The landscape around Weranga is generally flat to slightly undulating.
  10. Weranga is part of the Maranoa electorate.
