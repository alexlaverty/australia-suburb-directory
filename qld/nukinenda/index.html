Facts about NUKINENDA

  1. Nukinenda is a rural locality in the Bundaberg Region, Queensland, Australia.
  2. In the 2016 census, Nukinenda had a population of 145 people.
  3. Nukinenda is located approximately 30km southwest of Bundaberg.
  4. The name Nukinenda is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word.
  5. Sugarcane farming is a prominent industry in the Nukinenda area.
  6. The locality is situated within the Burnett River catchment.
  7. Nukinenda experiences a humid subtropical climate.
  8. The area is known for its fertile agricultural land.
  9. Nukinenda is close to the locality of Gooburrum, which has a state school.
  10. The closest major town to Nukinenda is Bundaberg, offering amenities and services.
