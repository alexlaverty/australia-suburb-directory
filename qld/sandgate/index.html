Facts about SANDGATE

  1. Sandgate is a coastal suburb located 16km north of Brisbane CBD.
  2. The suburb is bordered by the waters of Moreton Bay to the east.
  3. Sandgate's population is approximately 9,000 residents.
  4. The area was traditionally inhabited by the Ningy Ningy people.
  5. Sandgate was first settled by Europeans in the 1850s.
  6. The town was initially a seaside resort for Brisbane residents.
  7. Sandgate Pier, a popular landmark, was first built in 1882.
  8. The suburb features a variety of heritage-listed buildings.
  9. Sandgate has a mix of residential, commercial, and recreational areas.
  10. The Brighton Hotel, a local landmark, was established in 1887.
