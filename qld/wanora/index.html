Facts about WANORA

  1. Wanora is located in the Somerset Region of Queensland, Australia.
  2. It is situated approximately 50km west of Brisbane.
  3. The name Wanora is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning 'resting place'.
  4. The area was traditionally home to the Jagera and Yuggera Aboriginal peoples.
  5. Wanora is known for its rural landscape and proximity to the Brisbane River.
  6. The area was once a significant producer of dairy and agricultural products.
  7. Wanora railway station, opened in 1887, served as an important transport hub for the region.
  8. The Wanora Country Market is a popular local event.
  9. The nearby Lake Wivenhoe offers recreational activities such as fishing and boating.
  10. Wanora has a mix of residential, rural, and industrial areas.
