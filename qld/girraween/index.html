Facts about GIRRAWEEN

  1. Girraween is a suburb of Brisbane, Queensland, Australia.
  2. It is located approximately 12 kilometers south-west of the Brisbane CBD.
  3. The name "Girraween" is derived from an Aboriginal word meaning "place of flowers."
  4. The suburb was originally part of the Sherwood Shire, before being amalgamated into the City of Brisbane in 1925.
  5. Girraween is primarily a residential area.
  6. It features a mix of housing styles, including post-war homes and more modern constructions.
  7. The suburb is served by the Girraween railway station, providing access to the Brisbane City network.
  8. Girraween is home to several parks and reserves, including Girraween Park and Boundary Park.
  9. The local community is diverse, reflecting the multicultural nature of Brisbane.
  10. The median house price in Girraween has generally trended upwards over the past decade.
