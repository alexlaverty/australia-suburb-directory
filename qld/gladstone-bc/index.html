Facts about GLADSTONE BC

  1. Gladstone is located in Central Queensland, Australia.
  2. The traditional owners of the land are the Gooreng Gooreng people.
  3. Gladstone is a major industrial hub, particularly for the resources sector.
  4. The Port of Gladstone is one of the largest coal export ports in the world.
  5. Gladstone is situated on the southern end of the Great Barrier Reef.
  6. The city experiences a sub-tropical climate.
  7. Gladstone has a significant history related to the development of the Queensland coal and alumina industries.
  8. The city is home to a number of beaches and islands.
  9. Gladstone offers various recreational activities including fishing, boating, and watersports.
  10. The Auckland Point Lookout provides panoramic views of the city and harbor.
