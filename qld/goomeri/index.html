Facts about GOOMERI

  1. Goomeri is located in the Gympie Region of Queensland, Australia.
  2. The town is situated on the Burnett Highway, approximately 260km northwest of Brisbane.
  3. Goomeri's name is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning "resting place".
  4. The area was originally home to the Wakka Wakka Aboriginal people.
  5. Goomeri was established in the late 19th century, primarily as a service centre for the surrounding agricultural district.
  6. The town is known for its rich agricultural heritage, particularly in dairy farming and timber production.
  7. The Goomeri Pumpkin Festival, held annually in May, is a popular event attracting visitors from across the region.
  8. Goomeri has a number of heritage-listed buildings, reflecting its history.
  9. The town is surrounded by picturesque countryside, offering opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking and fishing.
  10. The Kinbombi Falls, located near Goomeri, are a popular natural attraction.
