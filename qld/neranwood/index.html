Facts about NERANWOOD

  1. Neranwood is located in the Scenic Rim Region of Queensland, Australia.
  2. It is situated near the Lamington National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  3. The area is known for its lush rainforests, waterfalls, and diverse wildlife.
  4. Neranwood experiences a subtropical climate.
  5. The suburb is popular for bushwalking and birdwatching.
  6. Neranwood is part of the Division 10 of the Scenic Rim Regional Council.
  7. The name Neranwood may derive from a local Indigenous word.
  8. The area was historically used for logging and dairy farming.
  9. Neranwood is a relatively small and sparsely populated suburb.
  10. Many properties in Neranwood offer scenic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
