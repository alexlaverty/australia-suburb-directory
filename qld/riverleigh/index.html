Facts about RIVERLEIGH

  1. Riverleigh is a rural locality in the North Burnett Region, Queensland, Australia.
  2. In the 2016 census, Riverleigh had a population of 63 people.
  3. The median age of the Riverleigh population was 52 years, 14 years above the national median of 38.
  4. Children aged 0 - 14 years made up 11.1% of the population and people aged 65 years and over made up 28.6% of the population.
  5. Of people in Riverleigh aged 15 years and over, 47.8% were married and 26.1% never married.
  6. The main industries of employment for people aged 15 years and over, usually resident in Riverleigh, were Beef Cattle Farming (62.5%), Sheep, Beef Cattle and Grain Farming (18.8%) and Coal Mining (6.3%).
  7. The median weekly personal income for people aged 15 years and over, usually resident in Riverleigh, was not stated.
  8. Of employed people in Riverleigh aged 15 years and over, 12.5% worked 1 to 15 hours, 25.0% worked 16 to 29 hours and 56.3% worked 30 to 44 hours.
  9. The most common industries of employment for persons aged 15 years and over, usually resident in Riverleigh were Agriculture, Forestry and Fishing at 75%, and Mining at 25%.
  10. The median weekly household income was not stated.
