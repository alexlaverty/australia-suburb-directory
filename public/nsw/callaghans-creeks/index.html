Facts about CALLAGHANS CREEKS

  1. Callaghans Creek is a locality within the Lake Macquarie City local government area.
  2. It is situated approximately 150km north of Sydney.
  3. The area is predominantly residential, with a mix of detached houses and smaller dwellings.
  4. Callaghans Creek is named after the creek that flows through it.
  5. The suburb is nestled amidst bushland, providing a natural setting.
  6. The creek itself is a tributary of Lake Macquarie.
  7. Callaghans Creek offers a peaceful lifestyle, away from the hustle and bustle of city life.
  8. The community is known for its friendly atmosphere.
  9. Local amenities include shops, schools, and parks.
  10. Public transport is available, connecting Callaghans Creek to surrounding suburbs and the city centre.
