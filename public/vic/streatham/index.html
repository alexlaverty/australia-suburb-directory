Facts about STREATHAM

  1. Streatham is located approximately 22 km northwest of Melbourne's CBD.
  2. The suburb is named after Streatham in south London, England.
  3. Streatham was primarily a farming area until the mid-20th century.
  4. The area experienced significant residential development in the post-war era.
  5. Streatham is part of the City of Moonee Valley local government area.
  6. The suburb is home to several parks and reserves, including Streatham Park.
  7. Streatham has a local shopping strip along Streatham Crescent.
  8. The suburb is served by public transport, including buses and trains.
  9. Streatham is close to major arterial roads, providing easy access to other parts of Melbourne.
  10. The median house price in Streatham has seen steady growth over the past decade.
