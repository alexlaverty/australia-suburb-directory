Facts about WEST BENDIGO

  1. West Bendigo is a suburb of Bendigo, Victoria, Australia.
  2. It is located approximately 4 km west of the Bendigo city centre.
  3. The suburb is primarily residential, with a mix of housing styles.
  4. West Bendigo is home to several schools, including West Bendigo Primary School and Catherine McAuley College.
  5. The area features recreational facilities such as the Ewing Park sporting precinct.
  6. The Bendigo Creek Trail runs through West Bendigo, offering walking and cycling paths.
  7. Public transport is available via bus routes connecting to the city centre.
  8. West Bendigo has a local shopping centre catering to residents' daily needs.
  9. The suburb is known for its relatively affordable housing compared to other areas of Bendigo.
  10. West Bendigo is part of the City of Greater Bendigo local government area.
