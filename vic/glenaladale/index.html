Facts about GLENALADALE

  1. Glenaladale is located approximately 10 km east of Melbourne's CBD.
  2. It is part of the City of Monash local government area.
  3. The suburb is primarily residential with a mix of housing styles.
  4. Glenaladale is close to several major roads, including the Monash Freeway.
  5. Public transport options include bus and train services.
  6. The suburb is home to a number of parks and reserves.
  7. Glen Waverley Secondary College, one of Victoria's largest secondary schools, is located nearby.
  8. The Glen shopping centre, a major retail hub, is also within close proximity.
  9. Glenaladale is named after an area in Scotland.
  10. The suburb has experienced significant growth in recent decades.
