Facts about BRENTFORD SQUARE

  1. Brentford Square is a small residential pocket located within the larger suburb of Forest Hill, Victoria, Australia.
  2. It is primarily known for its namesake park, Brentford Square Reserve.
  3. The area is characterised by its quiet, suburban streets and predominantly residential housing.
  4. Brentford Square is located within close proximity to Forest Hill Chase Shopping Centre.
  5. The suburb is well-serviced by public transport, with bus routes connecting it to surrounding areas and nearby train stations.
  6. Brentford Square Reserve features a playground, making it a popular spot for families with young children.
  7. The surrounding streets are known for their established gardens and tree-lined nature.
  8. Brentford Square is part of the City of Whitehorse local government area.
  9. The suburb is relatively close to Nunawading and Mitcham.
  10. Housing in Brentford Square primarily consists of single-family homes.
