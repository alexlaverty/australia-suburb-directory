Facts about FORBES

  1. Forbes is located in the Central West region of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. The town is situated on the Lachlan River.
  3. Forbes is known as the 'Heart of the Gold Country' due to its gold mining history.
  4. The town was named after Sir Francis Forbes, the first Chief Justice of New South Wales.
  5. Forbes is home to the Forbes Advocate, one of the oldest continuously published newspapers in Australia.
  6. The town hosts the annual Forbes Rodeo, one of the largest rodeos in the Southern Hemisphere.
  7. Lake Forbes, a large man-made lake, provides water storage and recreational opportunities.
  8. The Forbes Shire Council is the local government area.
  9. The area around Forbes is primarily agricultural, with sheep and cattle grazing, as well as wheat and other crops.
  10. Forbes is located approximately 390 km west of Sydney.
