Facts about POMPAPIEL

  1. Pompapiel is a locality in the Shire of Moorabool, Victoria, Australia.
  2. It is located approximately 70km west of Melbourne.
  3. The area is primarily agricultural.
  4. Pompapiel is situated near the Brisbane Ranges National Park.
  5. The Lerderderg River flows nearby.
  6. The name 'Pompapiel' is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word.
  7. The population of Pompapiel is very small.
  8. The area experiences a temperate climate.
  9. The landscape is characterized by rolling hills and open farmland.
  10. Gold was discovered in the region in the 19th century, although not specifically in Pompapiel itself.

Tourist Locations near POMPAPIEL

  2. Lerderderg State Park
