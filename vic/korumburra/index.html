Facts about KORUMBURRA

  1. Korumburra is located in South Gippsland, Victoria, Australia.
  2. It is situated approximately 150 km south-east of Melbourne.
  3. The name Korumburra is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning "blackfish".
  4. The town is surrounded by rolling hills and lush farmland.
  5. Coal mining was a significant industry in the Korumburra area in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
  6. The South Gippsland railway line runs through Korumburra.
  7. The town has a small but active community.
  8. Korumburra is known for its peaceful and rural atmosphere.
  9. The area experiences a temperate climate with four distinct seasons.
  10. Local industries include agriculture, forestry and tourism.
