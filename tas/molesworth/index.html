Facts about MOLESWORTH

  1. Molesworth is located in Tasmania's Central Highlands region.
  2. The area is known for its rugged terrain and natural beauty.
  3. Molesworth is situated along the Mole Creek, a tributary of the Mersey River.
  4. The town is small and primarily serves as a service center for the surrounding agricultural areas.
  5. The landscape is dominated by dolerite capped mountains and rolling hills.
  6. The area experiences a cool temperate climate.
  7. Molesworth is close to the Great Western Tiers, a significant mountain range.
  8. The economy is largely based on agriculture, including sheep farming and beef production.
  9. The town has a community hall and a general store.
  10. Molesworth is part of the Meander Valley Council area.

  2. Marakoopa Cave
  3. King Solomons Cave
