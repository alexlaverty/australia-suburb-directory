Facts about HOTHAM

  1. Hotham is a suburb of Darwin, Northern Territory, Australia.
  2. It is located within the local government area of the City of Darwin.
  3. The suburb is primarily residential.
  4. Hotham is close to Darwin International Airport.
  5. It is also near the Royal Darwin Hospital.
  6. The suburb experiences a tropical climate.
  7. Hotham is named after Lord Hotham, Governor of Victoria from 1854 to 1855.
  8. The suburb contains a mix of housing styles.
  9. It has some parklands and reserves.
  10. Several bus routes service the suburb.

  2. Darwin International Airport
  3. Charles Darwin University Casuarina Campus
