Facts about KINTORE

  1. Kintore is located in the Northern Territory of Australia.
  2. Kintore is approximately 530km west of Alice Springs.
  3. The community is situated near the Kintore Range.
  4. Kintore is predominantly inhabited by Aboriginal Australians.
  5. The main language spoken in Kintore is Pintupi Luritja.
  6. Kintore is known for its strong connection to traditional Aboriginal culture.
  7. The community has a rich history of art and storytelling.
  8. Kintore plays a significant role in preserving Indigenous languages and customs.
  9. The area around Kintore features unique desert landscapes.
  10. Kintore experiences a hot desert climate.
