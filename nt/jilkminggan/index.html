Facts about JILKMINGGAN

  1. Jilkminggan is an Aboriginal community located in the Katherine Region of the Northern Territory, Australia.
  2. The community is situated approximately 160km south-east of Katherine.
  3. Jilkminggan is on the Roper River.
  4. The traditional owners of the land are the Mangarrayi people.
  5. The main language spoken is Kriol, an English-based creole language.
  6. Jilkminggan has a rich history and culture.
  7. The community is known for its strong artistic traditions.
  8. The Jilkminggan Regional Arts Centre showcases local artwork.
  9. The community has its own school and health clinic.
  10. Jilkminggan is accessible by road, though conditions can vary depending on the season.
