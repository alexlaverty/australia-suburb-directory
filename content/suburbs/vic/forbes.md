facts:
- Forbes is located in the Central West region of New South Wales, Australia.
- The town is situated on the Lachlan River.
- Forbes is known as the 'Heart of the Gold Country' due to its gold mining history.
- The town was named after Sir Francis Forbes, the first Chief Justice of New South
  Wales.
- Forbes is home to the Forbes Advocate, one of the oldest continuously published
  newspapers in Australia.
- The town hosts the annual Forbes Rodeo, one of the largest rodeos in the Southern
  Hemisphere.
- Lake Forbes, a large man-made lake, provides water storage and recreational opportunities.
- The Forbes Shire Council is the local government area.
- The area around Forbes is primarily agricultural, with sheep and cattle grazing,
  as well as wheat and other crops.
- Forbes is located approximately 390 km west of Sydney.
