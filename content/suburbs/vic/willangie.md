facts:
- Willangie is located in the Rural City of Mildura local government area.
- The town is situated approximately 488km northwest of Melbourne.
- Willangie is a small agricultural community.
- The primary industries in Willangie are wheat and sheep farming.
- The Post Office opened on 1 July 1913.
- The name Willangie is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning 'scrubby
  place'.
- The Willangie State School opened in 1915 and closed in 1992.
- The town has a public hall which is used for community events.
- Willangie is located close to the Wyperfeld National Park.
- The area experiences a semi-arid climate with hot summers and mild winters.
