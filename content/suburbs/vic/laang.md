facts:
- Laang is a rural locality in Victoria, Australia.
- It is located in the Shire of Campaspe local government area.
- Laang is situated approximately 212 kilometers north of Melbourne.
- The area was traditionally home to the Yorta Yorta Aboriginal people.
- The name "Laang" is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word.
- The primary industries in Laang and the surrounding area are agriculture, particularly
  grain farming and sheep grazing.
- Laang Post Office opened on 1 July 1876.
- Laang once had a general store, hotel, and primary school but no facilities currently
  remain.
- The area is known for its flat terrain and open landscapes.
- The railway line through Laang closed in 1986.
