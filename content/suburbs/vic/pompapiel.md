- Pompapiel is a locality in the Shire of Moorabool, Victoria, Australia.
- It is located approximately 70km west of Melbourne.
- The area is primarily agricultural.
- Pompapiel is situated near the Brisbane Ranges National Park.
- The Lerderderg River flows nearby.
- The name 'Pompapiel' is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word.
- The population of Pompapiel is very small.
- The area experiences a temperate climate.
- The landscape is characterized by rolling hills and open farmland.
- Gold was discovered in the region in the 19th century, although not specifically
  in Pompapiel itself.
