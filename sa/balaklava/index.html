Facts about BALAKLAVA

  1. Balaklava is located approximately 60km north of Adelaide, South Australia.
  2. The town was named after the Battle of Balaclava in the Crimean War.
  3. Balaklava is situated in the Wakefield Regional Council area.
  4. The area is known for its agricultural production, particularly grain and livestock.
  5. Balaklava has a Mediterranean climate with hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters.
  6. The town's main street features several historic buildings, including the Balaklava Courthouse and the Balaklava Hotel.
  7. Balaklava is home to a number of sporting clubs, including football, netball, and cricket teams.
  8. The Balaklava Cup is a popular annual horse racing event.
  9. The Balaklava Show, held annually, showcases the region's agricultural and horticultural achievements.
  10. The town has a small but active community, with a range of local businesses and services.
