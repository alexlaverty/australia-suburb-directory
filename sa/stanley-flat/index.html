Facts about STANLEY FLAT

  1. Stanley Flat is located in the Clare Valley wine region of South Australia.
  2. The town is situated approximately 135 kilometers north of Adelaide.
  3. Stanley Flat is known for its scenic landscapes and rolling hills.
  4. The area's economy is primarily driven by agriculture, particularly viticulture and sheep farming.
  5. Stanley Flat is home to a number of historic buildings, including the Stanley Flat School.
  6. The town is surrounded by several conservation parks and nature reserves.
  7. Stanley Flat experiences a Mediterranean climate with warm, dry summers and cool, wet winters.
  8. The region is popular for outdoor activities such as hiking, cycling, and birdwatching.
  9. Stanley Flat hosts a variety of community events throughout the year, including the annual Clare Valley Gourmet Weekend.
  10. The town offers a peaceful and relaxed lifestyle for its residents.
