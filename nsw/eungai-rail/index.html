Facts about EUNGAI RAIL

  1. Eungai Rail is a small rural village located on the Mid North Coast of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. The village is situated approximately 35 kilometres south of Kempsey and 45 kilometres north of Macksville.
  3. Eungai Rail is part of the Nambucca Valley Council local government area.
  4. The name "Eungai" is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning "meeting of the waters".
  5. The "Rail" part of the name refers to the railway line that runs through the village.
  6. Eungai Rail is primarily a residential area with a small number of businesses.
  7. The village has a railway station on the North Coast railway line, providing passenger services to Sydney and Brisbane.
  8. Eungai Rail is surrounded by agricultural land and state forests.
  9. The area is known for its natural beauty, including the nearby Eungai Creek and the Nambucca River.
  10. The population of Eungai Rail is small, estimated to be several hundred residents.
