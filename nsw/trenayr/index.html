Facts about TRENAYR

  1. Trenayr is a rural locality situated on the Clarence River in New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is part of the Clarence Valley Council local government area.
  3. The name Trenayr may be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning 'meeting of the waters'.
  4. Trenayr is located approximately 15 kilometers south of Grafton.
  5. The area is primarily known for its agricultural land and river views.
  6. Trenayr Public School, established in 1882, serves the local community and surrounding areas.
  7. The Trenayr Hall is a community hub for events and gatherings.
  8. The locality is home to a mix of rural residential properties and working farms.
  9. The Clarence River at Trenayr is popular for fishing and watersports.
  10. Trenayr experiences a humid subtropical climate.
