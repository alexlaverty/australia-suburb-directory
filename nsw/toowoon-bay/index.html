Facts about TOOWOON BAY

  1. Toowoon Bay is located on the Central Coast of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is approximately 90km north of Sydney.
  3. The name "Toowoon Bay" is derived from an Aboriginal word meaning "deep water bay" or "place of the meeting of the waters."
  4. Toowoon Bay is known for its calm, sheltered beach, which is popular for swimming, paddleboarding, and kayaking.
  5. The bay is also a popular spot for fishing, with bream, whiting, and flathead commonly caught.
  6. Toowoon Bay is surrounded by bushland, providing opportunities for bushwalking and birdwatching.
  7. The suburb has a small village atmosphere with a few shops, cafes, and restaurants.
  8. Toowoon Bay is a popular holiday destination, particularly for families.
  9. The area around Toowoon Bay has been inhabited by Aboriginal people for thousands of years.
  10. Toowoon Bay is close to other popular Central Coast destinations such as The Entrance and Terrigal.
