Facts about TOORAWEENAH

  1. Tooraweenah is located in the central west region of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. The town is situated on the banks of the Macquarie River.
  3. Tooraweenah is approximately 40 kilometers north of Gilgandra.
  4. The name Tooraweenah is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning "meeting of the waters."
  5. The area is known for its agriculture, particularly sheep and cattle grazing.
  6. Tooraweenah has a small population, with fewer than 200 residents.
  7. The town experiences a semi-arid climate with hot summers and mild winters.
  8. Tooraweenah is part of the Warrumbungle Shire local government area.
  9. The economy of Tooraweenah is largely based on farming and related industries.
  10. The Tooraweenah War Memorial commemorates those who served in various wars.
