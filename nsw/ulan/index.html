Facts about ULAN

  1. Ulan is a small rural village in the Central West region of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is located within the Warrumbungle Shire local government area.
  3. The village is situated approximately 370 km northwest of Sydney.
  4. Ulan is known for its coal mining history and the nearby Ulan Coal Mine.
  5. The area around Ulan is primarily agricultural, with sheep and cattle farming being common.
  6. The landscape is characterized by rolling hills and open plains.
  7. The village has a small population, with limited amenities and services.
  8. The nearest larger town to Ulan is Mudgee, which offers a wider range of facilities.
  9. Ulan experiences a temperate climate with warm summers and cool winters.
  10. The name "Ulan" is believed to be derived from an Aboriginal word meaning "meeting of the waters."
