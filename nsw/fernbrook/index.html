Facts about FERNBROOK

  1. Fernbrook is a suburb located in the local government area of Camden Council in the state of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is situated approximately 62 kilometers southwest of the Sydney central business district.
  3. Fernbrook is primarily a residential area.
  4. The suburb is part of the Macarthur region.
  5. Fernbrook experiences a temperate climate with warm summers and cool winters.
  6. The area is known for its semi-rural character.
  7. Housing in Fernbrook predominantly consists of detached houses.
  8. Fernbrook is close to the town of Camden, which offers a range of amenities and services.
  9. The suburb is well-connected by road to surrounding areas.
  10. Public transport options in Fernbrook include bus services.
