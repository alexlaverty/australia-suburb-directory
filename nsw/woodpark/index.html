Facts about WOODPARK

  1. Woodpark is a suburb of Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is located 32 kilometers west of the Sydney central business district.
  3. Woodpark is in the local government area of the City of Blacktown.
  4. The suburb is predominantly residential.
  5. Woodpark is served by the Blacktown railway station.
  6. Woodpark Public School is located in the suburb.
  7. The suburb has a number of parks and reserves.
  8. Woodpark is close to the Prospect Reservoir.
  9. The area was originally home to the Darug people.
  10. The name Woodpark is derived from the name given by early settlers to the area.
