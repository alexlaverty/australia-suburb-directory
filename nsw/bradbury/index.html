Facts about BRADBURY

  1. Bradbury is located in the local government area of Campbelltown City Council.
  2. It is situated approximately 45 kilometers southwest of the Sydney central business district.
  3. The suburb was named after the first Australian recipient of the Victoria Cross, Edward Kenna, whose mother's maiden name was Bradbury.
  4. Bradbury was primarily developed in the 1960s and 1970s as part of the expansion of Sydney's southwestern suburbs.
  5. The suburb features a mix of housing styles, ranging from detached houses to townhouses and units.
  6. Bradbury Public School is the main primary school serving the local community.
  7. The suburb has a small shopping center that provides basic amenities to residents.
  8. Bradbury is located close to several major roads, including the Hume Highway and the M5 South Western Motorway.
  9. The suburb experiences a temperate climate with warm summers and mild winters.
  10. Bradbury is part of the Greater Western Sydney region.
