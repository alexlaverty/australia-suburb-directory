Facts about MOUNTAIN CREEK

  1. Mountain Creek is a suburb located in the Central Coast region of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is part of the Central Coast Council local government area.
  3. The suburb is situated between the suburbs of Erina and Berkeley Vale.
  4. Mountain Creek is primarily a residential area.
  5. The suburb is known for its natural beauty and proximity to the Brisbane Water National Park.
  6. Mountain Creek offers a quiet and peaceful lifestyle.
  7. The suburb is within commuting distance of Sydney.
  8. The suburb name is derived from the nearby Mountain Lagoon and its creek.
  9. Mountain Creek has a small shopping centre that caters to the local community.
  10. Housing in Mountain Creek consists mainly of detached houses and townhouses.
