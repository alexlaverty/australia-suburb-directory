Facts about YALBRAITH

  1. Yalbraith is a locality in the Riverina region of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is located approximately 6 kilometres (3.7 mi) southwest of the town of Leeton.
  3. The area is primarily agricultural, with rice, wheat, and other crops grown in the surrounding fields.
  4. Yalbraith is situated within the Leeton Shire Council local government area.
  5. The name 'Yalbraith' is thought to be derived from an Aboriginal word.
  6. The traditional owners of the land are the Wiradjuri people.
  7. The climate in Yalbraith is generally warm and dry, with hot summers and mild winters.
  8. The area is relatively flat, with the Murrumbidgee River flowing nearby.
  9. Yalbraith is a small community with a limited number of residents.
  10. The nearest major town, Leeton, provides access to essential services and amenities.
