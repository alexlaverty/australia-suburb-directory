Facts about EAST GRESFORD

  1. East Gresford is located in the Dungog Shire local government area.
  2. The suburb is situated on the Paterson River.
  3. East Gresford is approximately 220 kilometers north of Sydney.
  4. The area is known for its agricultural land and horse studs.
  5. East Gresford Public School is the primary school in the area.
  6. St. Anne's Anglican Church is a prominent landmark in East Gresford.
  7. The Gresford Show is an annual agricultural show held in the area.
  8. The Paterson River provides opportunities for fishing and watersports.
  9. East Gresford has a small community with a local shop and hotel.
  10. The surrounding area includes rolling hills and scenic countryside.
