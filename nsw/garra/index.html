Facts about GARRA

  1. Garra is a locality in the Riverina region of New South Wales, Australia.
  2. It is situated by road, about 17 kilometres (11 mi) west of Ardlethan and 22 kilometres (14 mi) east of Temora.
  3. Garra Post Office opened on 1 July 1924 and closed in 1973.
  4. The area around Garra is known for its agricultural activities.
  5. The name, Garra, was taken from the local Aboriginal tribe's word for "scrubby place" or "place of trees."
  6. The area now known as Garra was originally part of the vast pastoral holdings known as 'Narraburra' and 'North Yathong' Stations.
  7. The locality contains the Garra Cemetery.
  8. The primary economic activities in the Garra area are wheat and sheep farming.
  9. The closest town providing services like shops and schools is Ardlethan.
  10. Garra is located within the Bland Shire Council area.
