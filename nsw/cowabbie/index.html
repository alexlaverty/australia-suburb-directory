Facts about COWABBIE

  4. Cowabbie is situated near the Lachlan River.
  5. The area is primarily agricultural, with farming being the main industry.
  6. Cowabbie has a small community hall, which serves as a central hub for social gatherings.

  8. Cowabbie is known for its friendly and welcoming community.
  9. The surrounding landscape is characterized by open plains and grasslands.
  10. The climate in Cowabbie is generally dry and temperate.
