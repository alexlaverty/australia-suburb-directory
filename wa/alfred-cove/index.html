Facts about ALFRED COVE

  1. Alfred Cove is a suburb of Perth, Western Australia.
  2. It is located within the City of Melville local government area.
  3. The suburb is named after Alfred Waylen, an early settler in the area.
  4. Alfred Cove is primarily a residential area.
  5. The suburb is situated on the banks of the Swan River.
  6. Alfred Cove is home to the Royal Perth Yacht Club.
  7. The area offers views of the Perth city skyline.
  8. The suburb is known for its tranquil atmosphere.
  9. Alfred Cove has convenient access to public transport.
  10. The population of Alfred Cove is relatively small compared to other Perth suburbs.
