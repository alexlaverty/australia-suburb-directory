Facts about DURAWAH

  4. Durawah's primary industry is agriculture, particularly wheat farming.
  5. The area is known for its diverse flora and fauna.
  6. Durawah is located close to other small Wheatbelt towns, offering a sense of community.
  7. The region experiences hot, dry summers and cool, wet winters.
  8. The landscape of Durawah is generally flat, typical of the Wheatbelt region.
  9. Durawah is connected to the wider region via road networks.
  10. The local community often holds events and gatherings.
