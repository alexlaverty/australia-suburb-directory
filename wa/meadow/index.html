Facts about MEADOW

  1. Meadow Springs is a suburb of Mandurah, located approximately 75km south of Perth, Western Australia.
  2. The area was originally known as 'The Meadow', and the name 'Meadow Springs' was adopted in the late 1980s.
  3. A significant portion of Meadow Springs is situated on reclaimed swampland.
  4. Meadow Springs is bordered by the Peel Inlet to the east and the Indian Ocean to the west.
  5. The suburb features a golf course designed by Graham Marsh.
  6. Meadow Springs is home to several parks and reserves, including Meadow Springs Reserve and Samphire Cove Reserve.
  7. The suburb has a mix of housing options, including single-family homes, townhouses, and apartments.
  8. Meadow Springs is served by the Mandurah railway line, with a station located within the suburb.
  9. The population of Meadow Springs has grown significantly in recent decades.
  10. Meadow Springs is part of the City of Mandurah local government area.
