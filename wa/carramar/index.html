Facts about CARRAMAR

  1. Carramar is a suburb of Perth, Western Australia, located within the City of Wanneroo.
  2. The name Carramar is derived from the Aboriginal word meaning "boomerang".
  3. Carramar is primarily a residential suburb.
  4. Development of Carramar began in the 1990s.
  5. Carramar is home to several parks and reserves, including Carramar Park and the Carramar Golf Course.
  6. The suburb has a mix of housing styles, from traditional single-family homes to modern apartments.
  7. Carramar is located close to major transport routes, including the Mitchell Freeway and Wanneroo Road.
  8. The suburb is within close proximity to shopping centers, such as Lakeside Joondalup Shopping City.
  9. Carramar has a relatively young population.
  10. Carramar experiences a Mediterranean climate.
