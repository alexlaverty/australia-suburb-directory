Facts about HALLS HEAD

  1. Halls Head is a coastal suburb located in the City of Mandurah, Western Australia.
  2. It is situated on a peninsula bounded by the Peel Inlet and the Indian Ocean.
  3. Halls Head is approximately 70 kilometers south of Perth.
  4. The suburb is named after Captain Henry Hall, who explored the area in 1829.
  5. Halls Head is known for its beautiful beaches, including Halls Head Beach and Blue Bay Beach.
  6. The suburb offers a range of recreational activities such as swimming, fishing, boating, and golfing.
  7. Halls Head has a variety of shops, restaurants, and cafes.
  8. The suburb is popular with families and retirees.
  9. Halls Head has experienced significant growth in recent years.
  10. The median house price in Halls Head is relatively high compared to other suburbs in Mandurah.
