Facts about KUNJIN

  1. Kunjin is a small town located in the Wheatbelt region of Western Australia.
  2. It is situated approximately 320km east of Perth.
  3. The town is located within the Shire of Dalwallinu.
  4. Kunjin's name is derived from an Aboriginal word, the meaning of which is uncertain, possibly meaning "place of the plains kangaroo".
  5. The primary industry in Kunjin is agriculture, particularly wheat farming.
  6. The surrounding areas also support sheep grazing.
  7. Kunjin experiences a semi-arid climate with hot summers and cool winters.
  8. The town's population is very small, with fewer than 100 residents.
  9. Kunjin has limited facilities, with residents relying on nearby towns for services.
  10. The main road through Kunjin is the Great Eastern Highway.
