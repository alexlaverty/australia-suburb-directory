Facts about LOWDEN

  1. Lowden is a small town located in the Wheatbelt region of Western Australia.
  2. It is situated approximately 221 kilometres east of Perth.
  3. The town is located within the Shire of Kellerberrin local government area.
  4. Lowden is primarily an agricultural area, with wheat and sheep farming being prominent activities.
  5. The name Lowden is of Scottish origin.
  6. The townsite was gazetted in 1923.
  7. Lowden experienced significant growth during the early 20th century due to the expansion of the railway.
  8. The population of Lowden has declined in recent decades.
  9. Lowden has a small number of community facilities, including a hall and a recreation ground.
  10. The surrounding landscape is characterized by gently undulating hills and open farmland.
